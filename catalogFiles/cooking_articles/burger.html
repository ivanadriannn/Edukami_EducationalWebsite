Burger

Burger is a type of dish consisting of minced meat which is formed into a patty, then grilled, fried, or grilled, and placed inside a round bread commonly called a bun. Burgers are usually served with a variety of additional ingredients such as cheese, sauce, vegetables, and pickles to add a variety of flavors and textures.

Ingredients
  • Ground chuck 1 1/2 pounds
  • Kosher salt
  • Black Pepper grounded
  • Canola oil 1 1/2 tablespoons
  • Cheese 4 slices
  • Hamburger buns 4 Pieces

Steps

1. Divide the meat into 4 equal portions (about 6 ounces each). Form each portion loosely into a 3/4-inch-thick burger and make a deep depression in the center with your thumb.
2. Season both sides of each burger with salt and pepper.
3. Brush the burgers with the oil. Grill the burgers until golden brown and slightly charred on the first side, about 3 minutes for beef.
