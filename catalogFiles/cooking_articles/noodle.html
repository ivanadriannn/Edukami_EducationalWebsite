Fried Noodle

Fried noodle is a culinary dish consisting of noodles that are cooked first and then fried together with other ingredients and spices. This dish is popular in many countries in Asia, such as China, Indonesia, Malaysia and Thailand.

Ingredients
  • Noodle 1 pack
  • Water 750 ml
  • Sweet soy sauce 7 tbsp
  • Olive oil 4 tbsp
  • Garlic 6 cloves
  • Salt 2 tsp
  • Eggs 3
  • White ground pepper 2 tbsp
  • Chicken stock powder 1 cube
  • Bok choy 1 pack
  • Red onion 1
  • Candle nut 6 piece
  • Scallion 4 stalks

Steps

1. Prepare a pan with 750 ml water and boil the egg noodle for about 2 or 3 minutes add olive oil and stir it
2. Blend the seasoning : onion, garlic, chili, and candlenut
3. Scramble the eggs with a little salt and set aside
