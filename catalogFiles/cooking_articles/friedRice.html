Ingredients
  • Shallots 2 cloves
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Curly red chili 2 pieces
  • Red/green chili 1 Large
  • Green onions to taste
  • Fish sauce 1 tablespoon
  • Oyster sauce 1/2 tablespoon
  • Sweet soy sauce 1 tablespoon
  • Soy sauce 1/2 teaspoon
  • Pepper 1/4 teaspoon
  • Flavoring 1/2 teaspoon
  • Margarine to taste

Steps

1. Grind the shallots and garlic
2. Curly chili slices, large chili and meatballs. Size according to taste.
3. Heat the oil. Then fry the eggs by scrambling.
