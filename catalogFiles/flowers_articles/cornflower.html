Cornflower

Cornflower, also known as bachelor's button or Centaurea cyanus, is a flowering plant native to Europe and known for its beautiful blue flowers. This plant belongs to the Asteraceae family.

Recommended

Best Time to Plant : Between March and May
Growing Conditions : in well drained soil in full sun.

To know
  • Botanical Name: Centaurea cyanus

  • Common Name: Bachelor's button

  • Zones: 2 To 11

  • Bloom Time: Flower within ~10 weeks

Caring

1. Rake the soil to a fine tilth (so it's a fine, crumbly soil) and make some drills 12mm deep. The rows should be spaced about 30cm apart.
2. Water the soil before sowing and allow the drill to drain if the soil is dry, sprinkle the seed in carefully and cover up with soil
3. Make sure the space you are going to sow is weed free, by using a trowel to remove the weeds.
