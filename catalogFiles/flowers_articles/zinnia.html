Zinnia

Zinnias are a popular type of flowering plant in horticulture. This plant belongs to the genus Zinnia and the Asteraceae family. Zinnia is native to Central America and South America.

Recommended

Best Time to Plant : Between April and May
Growing Conditions : In well-drained soil

To know
  • Botanical Name: Zinnia violacea

  • Common Name: Elegant zinnia

  • Zones: 3 To 10

  • Bloom Time: Flower within ~2 Months

Caring

1. Plant zinnia seeds a few inches apart in rows or clumps. Thin to 8 to 18 inches apart
2. Zinnias need moist soil to grow their best. This is especially true of young plants. Water deeply a few times a week so the soil stays moist 6 to 8 inches deep.
3. Deadhead (trim) spent flowers regularly to promote more flowering and to keep the plant tidy.
