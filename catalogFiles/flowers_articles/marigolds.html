Marigolds

Marigolds, or Tagetes in scientific language, is a genus of flowering plants consisting of several species belonging to the Asteraceae family. This plant originates from Central America and South America, but has now spread throughout the world as an ornamental plant.

Recommended

Best Time to Plant : in the spring
Growing Conditions : full sun and a fertile soil.

To know
  • Botanical Name: Tagetes erecta

  • Common Name: Poet's marigold

  • Zones: 2 To 11

  • Bloom Time: Flower within ~8 Weeks

Caring

1. Sow the seed thinly into moist well-prepared soil and thin the seedlings to 10-20 cm apart, depending on variety size.
2. Removing the dead heads encourages more flowers to be produced, while pinching out shoots of tall varieties can encourage bushy growth.
3. Marigolds need free-draining soil and, if the ground is heavy and poorly drained, they may develop grey mould or root rots.
