Dianthus

Dianthus is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. This plant has about 300 species spread in various regions around the world, including Europe, Asia, and North America. Dianthus is known for its beautiful and fragrant flowers.

Recommended

Best Time to Plant : Early spring
Growing Conditions : In well-drained soil

To know
  • Botanical Name: Dianthus caryophyllus

  • Common Name: Carnation

  • Zones: 4 To 10

  • Bloom Time: Flower within ~5 Months

Caring

1. Space dianthus about 6 to 18 inches apart, depending on the type; dig a hole roughly twice the size of the root ball.
2. Although it's prone to fungal root rot, you can avoid this issue with well-drained soil and appropriate watering.
3. Overwatering and inadequate air circulation can lead to root rot and other fungal diseases in dianthus.
