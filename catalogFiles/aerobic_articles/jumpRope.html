Jump Rope

Jump rope, also known as skipping, is the activity of practicing or playing using a rope that is swung by means of a jump above it. In a jump rope, a person jumps through a rope that is swung in front of and behind the body at a consistent speed.

Recommended

~5 minutes of Jump Rope
Three times every week

Breaststroke

1. Choose the right rope. You should find a rope that reaches nearly up to your shoulders when it's folded in half.
2. Hold the rope's handles in each of your hands. Extend your hands and forearms at least a foot away from your body, at a 45 degree angle.
3. The rope should hang behind you, so that the middle of the rope hits the back of your feet.
