Cycling

Cycling, also known as biking, is the activity of using a bicycle to move from one place to another or as a form of recreation and exercise. In cycling, a person uses the strength of the muscles of the body, especially the legs, to pedal the bicycle and move the wheels.

Breaststroke

1. Find a fitting location. When you’re learning as a beginner, you want to find a place that’s comfortable and far from traffic.
2. Test the brakes. Find out how the brakes work on the bicycle. Stay off the bicycle. Keep it next to you and walk it.
3. Pedal up the slope. From the flat bottom of the hill, start pedaling. The slope requires additional work. Lean forward into the pedaling or even stand up to gain extra power.
