Running

Running is a form of physical activity in which a person runs at a certain speed. It involves repeated rapid movements using the feet as the prime mover. Running can be done in places such as roads, running tracks, treadmills, or open areas such as parks.

Recommended

~45 minutes of Running
Three times every week

Benefits
  • Help to build strong bones
  • Strengthen Legs
  • Can be meditative
  • Increases Heart Rate
  • Great for your heart and lungs
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Burns calories
  • improve cardiovascular fitness

Breaststroke

1. Keep Your Shoulders Down. Focus on keeping them down–and away from your ears!
2. Set your gaze straight ahead of you, looking around 20 to 30 meters out. Don’t look at your feet unless you’re doing a short drill
3. Relax your hands to ease tension on your shoulders and back
