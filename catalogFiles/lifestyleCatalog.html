Cooking
Cooking is the process of preparing food by applying heat, usually through various techniques such as baking, boiling, frying, grilling, or steaming. It involves combining ingredients, following recipes or personal creativity, and using culinary skills to transform raw or semi-processed ingredients into flavorful and enjoyable dishes. Cooking can be done for various purposes, including nourishment, enjoyment, cultural traditions, or professional culinary endeavors. It is a fundamental aspect of human civilization and an art form that allows individuals to express their creativity and satisfy their culinary desires.
